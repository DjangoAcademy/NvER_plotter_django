Embryogenesis and Regeneration Mfuzz clusters

Using M-Fuzz clustering algorithms (Ref), we have regrouped temporal expression patterns during embryonic, larval and post-metamorphic development as well as during the process of sub-pharyngeal oral Regeneration of Nematostella vectensis. By clicking on the cluster representing the expression profiles of interest, you will be able to scroll through all associated genes . The checkboxes on the left will enable you to visualize the specific expression pattern of a given gene (up to five genes simultaneously, max) and by clicking on the highlighted accessions numbers you will be directed to the best BLAST hit.

References for regeneration and novel embryonic datasets: Warner et al(a), 2017, Warner et al(b), 2017. Re-analyzed embryonic datasets: Helm et al. 2013, Tulin et al. 2013
